# Intended Use Statement

QuDrugForge is a research-use computational decision-support platform for oncology drug-discovery planning.

It shortlists computational hit hypotheses, generates evidence dossiers, prioritizes wet-lab assay plans, and learns from imported experimental feedback. It is not intended to diagnose disease, select patient treatment, claim clinical efficacy, establish human safety, or replace qualified scientific, regulatory, or medical review.

All ranked molecules remain computational hypotheses until independently synthesized or procured, analytically confirmed, tested in validated assays, reviewed for ADMET and selectivity, and governed under the sponsor's quality system.
