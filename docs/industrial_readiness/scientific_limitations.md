# Scientific Limitations

QuDrugForge does not prove target engagement, potency, selectivity, safety, efficacy, clinical utility, or regulatory suitability.

Key limitations:
- AlphaFold structures may not represent assay-ready conformations.
- Docking poses are computational hypotheses and may fail under redocking or experimental validation.
- ADMET/tox predictions are model estimates and endpoint coverage may be incomplete.
- Quantum/QML scoring is exploratory unless validated prospectively.
- Wet-lab results require QC review before model feedback is trusted.
- Advanced physics such as explicit-solvent MD/FEP is labelled as planned unless fully configured.
