# Competitive Landscape

Position QuDrugForge as a research-use computational decision-support workstation, not a clinical decision product.

Differentiators:
- Candidate evidence dossiers with explicit claim boundaries.
- Multi-engine docking plus visual QA and artifact provenance.
- Wet-lab assay packet export and closed-loop assay-result import.
- Governance primitives: decision gates, e-signature records, frozen reports, and audit export.
- Oncology-first target, receptor, ChEMBL, ADMET, and safety-panel workflow.
