# Validation Case Study: PIK3CA

Goal: validate PI3K-alpha computational triage and isoform selectivity planning for breast/prostate oncology research contexts.

Reference comparator: alpelisib.

Critical assays: PI3K-alpha biochemical IC50, PI3K isoform selectivity, pAKT pathway engagement, matched cell-line viability, hERG/CYP, solubility, and permeability.
