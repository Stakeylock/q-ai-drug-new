# Validation Case Study: EGFR

Goal: demonstrate that EGFR candidate ranking produces reproducible computational dossiers and wet-lab assay plans without therapeutic overclaiming.

Minimum evidence package:
- EGFR receptor provenance and docking pose sources.
- Reference inhibitor comparators such as osimertinib, erlotinib, and gefitinib.
- Docking, ADMET, selectivity, uncertainty, and applicability-domain labels.
- Biochemical IC50/Ki, Kd engagement, pEGFR pathway, viability, hERG/CYP, solubility, and permeability assay plan.
- Imported assay results and recalibration summary when available.
