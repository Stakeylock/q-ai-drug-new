# Wet-Lab Validation Plan

## Compound Access

- Check compound procurement or synthesis feasibility.
- Confirm identity, purity, salt form, and stability.
- Prioritize a small number of top candidates with clean medchem and ADMET triage.

## Biochemical Validation

- Run target-specific biochemical assay for EGFR, PARP1, or PIK3CA.
- Measure dose-response IC50 with replicates.
- Include reference inhibitors and inactive controls.
- Use an orthogonal assay to reduce assay-artifact risk.

## Cellular Validation

- Select cancer cell lines aligned to target biology and mutation context.
- Run target-engagement or pathway modulation readouts where possible.
- Run cytotoxicity assay to separate target-specific activity from general toxicity.

## Selectivity Counterscreens

- EGFR: counterscreen related kinases such as HER2, ALK, BRAF, and mutant/wild-type contexts as appropriate.
- PARP1: counterscreen PARP2 and tankyrase family where possible.
- PIK3CA: counterscreen PI3K beta, delta, and gamma isoforms.

## ADMET Follow-Up

- hERG inhibition.
- AMES mutagenicity.
- DILI risk.
- CYP inhibition and induction.
- Solubility and permeability.
- Microsomal stability and clearance.

## Hit Confirmation Criteria

- Reproducible biochemical dose-response.
- Orthogonal assay confirmation.
- Cellular evidence consistent with target biology.
- Acceptable early selectivity and toxicity profile.
- Synthetic tractability and purity confirmed.
